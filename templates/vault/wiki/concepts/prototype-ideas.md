# Prototype ideas — AI builder session backlog

The idea backlog for the team's recurring **AI builder session** (rapid, ambitious prototyping with AI — not BAU, not sprint work). Open this page at the start of the session; run `/spark` beforehand to get ranked candidates. Built prototypes flip to `built`, get a brag entry, and link back here.

## The session

> *Replace this callout with your team's own session brief and ground rules. The defaults that work well: no BAU work, new builds only; aim ambitious (if you'd be slightly intimidated to attempt it in a normal sprint, it's the right target); something to show at the end, polished or not.*

## How entries work

One entry per idea. Keep it to a few lines:

- **Pitch** — one sentence, what you'd build.
- **Pain** — wikilink to the concept/ticket/incident that surfaced it. Every idea should trace to real friction.
- **Ambition** — `comfortable | stretch | intimidating`. Per the ground rules, prefer `stretch`+.
- **Status** — `seed | built | shipped | dead`. When built, link the demo/repo/brag entry.

New seeds arrive two ways, both standing rules in the schema: spotted in passing while any agent reads code, and swept up by `/capture` at the end of any session that touched a service.
